Task 4 — General Questions:
 Declaration: Create a variable. 
 Initialisation: assign first value;
 Re-assignment: change value.

2. Scopes:
   Global: accessible everywhere.
   Function/Local: accessible inside a function.
   Block: accessible inside {} with let or const.
3. Redeclare let or const:
    Causes SyntaxError.

4. Use a variable before declaration:
     var: undefined.
     let/const: ReferenceError.

5. When to use var:
Rarely, only for old browser support or function-scoped variables; prefer let / const.
